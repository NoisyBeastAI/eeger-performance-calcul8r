Methodology Impressions are derived from budget ÷ CPM × 1,000 by channel. Visits come from channel CTR. Registrations use channel-specific visit → registration rates rather than one blended site CVR across all traffic sources. Profiles and applications are then applied sequentially.
Show the underlying logic

Paid registrations are reported separately from organic registrations so the forecast does not overstate incremental impact. The total registration view adds both together for overall platform volume. Monthly pacing uses an S-curve to reflect a slower launch period before stabilising into always-on activity.
